My Projects and Researches

Q-Diet: Personalized Nutrition Planner

I developed the idea for Q-Diet, a personalized nutrition planning tool designed to help users make informed dietary choices based on their unique needs and health goals. The project explores the intersection of chemical engineering and health science, focusing on data-driven nutrition, ingredient analysis, and meal planning optimization.

Velcro: Research & Applications

Conducted in-depth research on the properties, chemical composition, and innovative applications of Velcro. My work analyzed the molecular interactions behind Velcro’s adhesion, its real-world uses, and the scope for sustainable material alternatives in manufacturing.

Assessing Campus Sustainability: Greenness of Reyansh College of Hotel

Performed a comprehensive evaluation of the environmental sustainability (greenness) at Reyansh College of Hotel Management. This involved auditing energy consumption, waste management, and green practices, and providing actionable recommendations for further sustainability improvements.

Pune’s Carbon Footprint Analysis

Calculated and analyzed the total carbon footprint generated by the city of Pune. The project required collecting local data, estimating emission sources, and applying chemical engineering methodologies to quantify environmental impact—contributing to awareness and policymaking for a more sustainable urban future.
